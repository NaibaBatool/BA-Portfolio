# Superstore Sales Performance Dashboard

## Objective
Analyse US retail sales data to identify which regions, categories, 
and customer segments drive the most profit.

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel 2016
- Power Query (data cleaning and transformation)
- Pivot Tables (analysis)
- Excel Charts (visualisation)

## Dataset
Sample Superstore dataset — 2,676 orders across 2015–2018.
Source: Kaggle

## Key Findings
- West region leads in sales ($180k) but East is more profitable (15.6% margin)
- Technology category has the highest profit margin at 20%
- Furniture has the worst margin at 10% despite being second highest in sales
- Consumer segment drives 47% of total revenue
- Business grew 81% from 2015 to 2017 before plateauing in 2018
